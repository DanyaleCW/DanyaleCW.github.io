My Design Process

I start my process by determining the problem I am trying to solve. Once I know the problem(s), complete a competitive set and audit to try to figure out what similars site did well and what they did not. There is no point in trying to reinvent the wheel. The site is then mapped and color and typography are determined. The next steps are mood boards, wireframes and low resolution mockups. Then it is time to start building the pages in Adobe XD...
