─ Case Study

I designed the concept site for The Present because the site needed to be brought into the 21st century with a sleek new design. I decided on rich photographs that will enage the users, a modern layout, and an in-site registration andpayment page which will allow customer to sign-up without having to leave the site which will increase engagement and revenue. This is my first attempt at a site redesign.
